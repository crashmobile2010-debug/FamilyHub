🎧 Play through a speaker

Music follows whatever this device's audio is connected to:

1. Open this device's Bluetooth settings (swipe down from the top on Android / Control Centre on iPad).

2. Put your speaker or amp in pairing mode and tap it in the list.

3. Come back and press play — the hub's audio moves to the speaker automatically.

Already paired once? Just tap the speaker's name to reconnect.

Settings
